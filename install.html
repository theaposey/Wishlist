Wishlist is a free iPhone app — no App Store needed.
Install it in Safari in under a minute.

1

Open this page in Safari

Make sure you're using Safari — Chrome and other browsers can't install apps on iPhone. If you're not in Safari, copy the URL and paste it there.

2

Tap the Share button

The box with an arrow pointing up — in the bottom toolbar in Safari.

3

Tap "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down in the share sheet and tap Add to Home Screen. Change the name if you like, then tap Add in the top-right corner.

✓

Open Wishlist from your home screen

It runs like a native app — full screen, no browser chrome, works offline. Your wishlist data stays private on your device.

You'll need to open the Wishlist URL in Safari first — ask whoever shared this wishlist with you for the link, or visit the app directly.
